My top 3 movies of 2023

Super Mario Bros

A great option that is fun for families. A delight to children and nostalgia trip for parents.

John Wick: Chapter 4

A heart pounding action movie to the nth degree!

The Pale Blue Eye

Christian Bale does it again portraying an emotionally wounded detective commissioned by West Point to solve a murder of one of thier students. An emotional story that keeps you guessing to the very end and is aided by Bales' protoge, Edgar Allen Poe.
